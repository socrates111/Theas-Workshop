Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how and why we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

1. Who We Are

This website is operated by Tea Gzirishvili. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and personal data.

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you use our website:

  • Your name and email address when you send us a message through our contact form
  • Your IP address and browser type (for analytics purposes)
  • Any additional data you voluntarily provide (e.g. art catalog requests)

3. Why We Collect Your Data

We collect your personal data in order to:

  • Respond to inquiries and messages you send via our contact form
  • Send you a requested catalog or exhibition information
  • Improve our website’s usability and design
  • Comply with legal requirements and protect against fraud or abuse

4. Email Usage

If you provide your email address, we may use it to:

  • Respond to your message or inquiry
  • Send information about exhibitions or new works (only if you have opted in)

We do not sell or share your email with third parties. You can request to have your email deleted at any time.

5. Cookies

This website uses cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies help us understand how users interact with the website. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Data Storage and Security

We store your data securely and use encryption and secure servers where applicable. We take appropriate measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of your data.

7. Third-party Services

We may use third-party services (such as Google Fonts or analytics tools) which may collect anonymous usage data. These services follow their own privacy policies.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise your rights, please contact us using the information below.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us.

10. Updates

This policy may be updated in the future. We encourage you to review this page from time to time.
